Nutrition Guide

Sample Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Protein smoothie or Overnight oats with chia
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken or fish with veggies and quinoa or brown rice
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries or mixed nuts
  • Dinner: Lean protein (salmon, turkey) with sweet potatoes and steamed greens

Hydration & Supplements

  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily
  • Optional: Multivitamin, Omega-3s, Protein Powder

Nutrition is key to sculpting your summer body. Stick to whole, nutrient-rich foods and stay consistent!
