6-Week Workout Plan

Weeks 1–2: Foundation & Conditioning

  • Mon/Wed/Fri – Strength: Squats, Push-ups, Rows, Glute Bridges, Planks
  • Tue/Thu – Cardio & Abs: 30-min cardio, Crunches, Russian Twists

Weeks 3–4: Intensity Increase

  • Mon/Wed/Fri – Circuits: Lunges, Burpees, Shoulder Press, Mountain Climbers, Side Planks
  • Tue/Thu – HIIT & Core: Sprints, Bicycle Crunches, Leg Raises

Weeks 5–6: Peak Performance

  • Mon/Wed/Fri – Advanced Circuits: Deadlifts, Push-up to Row, Jump Squats, Reverse Lunges, Plank Jacks
  • Tue/Thu – Endurance & Core: 40-min cardio, Planks, Flutter Kicks

Saturday: Active Recovery (yoga/stretching)
Sunday: Full Rest
